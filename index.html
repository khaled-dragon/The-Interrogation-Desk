Detective's Handbook
How to Play
🎯
The Objective
You are a detective. Across the desk sits a suspect — one of 10 different cases, picked randomly every game.

Your mission: push the suspect's Stress Level to 100 to extract a full confession — before the 10-minute timer runs out.
📊
Watch the Meters
Three bars tell you how the interrogation is going:
Stress
75%
⬆ Get this to 100 to win
Trust
45%
⬆ Higher trust = easier confession
Defense
30%
⬆ Too high = suspect shuts down ❌
🕹️
How Actions Work
After every response from the suspect, 3 choices appear at the bottom. Pick one — each choice is a fully written action your detective takes. Choices rotate and refresh so you never see the same set twice.
❓
Question
Ask a direct question. Gradually raises stress and unlocks evidence as you dig deeper.
👊
*SLAM*
Slam the desk for a big stress spike. Overuse raises the Defense meter fast — use sparingly!
👁️
Stare in silence
Pure psychological pressure. Slow but steady. Triggers micro-expressions like eye twitching.
🤫
Threatening whisper
Lean in close with a calculated threat. High impact. Drops the music to an eerie low tone.
📁
Show the Evidence
Appears as a choice once evidence is unlocked. Each piece triggers a unique, case-specific reaction. The most powerful move in the game.
🗂️
Unlocking Evidence
Evidence unlocks automatically as you keep questioning. Once found, it appears as a choice or you can click it directly from the top-left panel.
